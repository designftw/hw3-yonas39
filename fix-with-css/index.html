Graphic Design

Usablity

Learnablity

To improve the Learnablity of the website. I used CSS display mode and hid some repetitive elements from the home page. Besides making the website readable, I also made it easier for the users to find the food items on the home page without scrolling down. Since it's a pizza shop website, users come here to order food, so providing the food items at the top increases the learnability of the website.

Efficiency

I created an order here button that will scroll up and down along the user's movement everywhere in the home and about us page. It helps reduce the time the user spends finding the order here button.

Safety

I planned to add a safety feature that brings the user back home from the menu or discount pages. Unfortunately, once I get to the menu or discount pages, I cannot right-click to find the options to inspect elements. But my idea was to create a button redirecting users to the home page.

Graphics Design

I applied the principle of Graphics alignment throughout the page. I chose to align all the texts to the center. I also changed the background image to improve contrast and to make it more appealing. The color choice was another contrast technique I used to create consistency throughout the page. I assumed the color I chose was associated with the company's theme. Repetition is another graphic design principle I incorporated into my account. I used the round image display method and center text alignment in the website's home page and about us section. In addition, I also used the background effect on both pages(about and home).
